🎮 How to Play

  • Controls: ↑↓ keys or touch to move. In 2-player mode, Player 2 uses W/S keys.
  • Game Modes: Single Player, 2-Player Local, Tournament (3 rounds), Survival (avoid missing).
  • Power-ups: ⚡Speed boost, 📏Paddle size, ⚪Multi-ball, 🐌Slow motion.
  • Ball Skins: Classic, Neon (glowing), Fire (red trail), Ice (blue trail).
  • Themes: Default, Neon Retro, Dark Mode, Ocean Blue.
  • Scoring: First to 3 points with 2-point lead wins (except Survival mode).
